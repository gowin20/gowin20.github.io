Banff

I went to Banff National Park in Alberta with my family, in the summer of 2019.

The above picture is on the C Level Cirque Trail, which has an elevation gain of 2,477 feet. You can almost see our car along the shore of the lake!



The "Ink Pots." These hotsprings are unique because bubbles rise from them in perfectly circular patterns. If you enhance this image, you can see several circles of various sizes underwater, formed by geothermal vents.
